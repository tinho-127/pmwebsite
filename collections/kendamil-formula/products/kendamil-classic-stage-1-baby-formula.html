Kendamil Classic Stage 1 (800g) Baby Formula Formula Mixing Instructions

  • 01

    Always wash and dry hands before preparing baby‚Äôs meal. Wash and sterilize all bottles, nipples, and caps by boiling them for 3 to 5 minutes.

  • 02

    Boil water to 212 degrees Fahrenheit ( 50 degrees C). Pour half of the water into a bottle for an individual serving.

  • 03

    Fill the scoop and level (with the back of a clean knife). Only the enclosed scoop should be used. Add the entire quantity of milk powder indicated on the table to the water.

  • 04

    Shake thoroughly, add the rest of the water, and shake again. Allow cooling to 98.6 degrees Fahrenheit ( 37 degrees C) before feeding.

Important Feeding Information

  • Each baby formula uses different mixing proportions. Only use the scoop that is included in your formula.
  • Only add water and scoops at the ratios intended by the manufacturer. Do not supplement the milk feeds with any additional ingredients.
  • Do not prepare feeds in advance. In order to maintain maximum nutritional value, use the feeds immediately and dispose of any leftovers feed.
  • Once prepared, feed should be used within 2 hours if stored at room temperature. Bottles cooled with a cold ice pack should be used within 4 hours. Refrigerated bottles may be used for up to 24 hours.
  • Microwaves heat unevenly! Do not microwave prepared feeds as heat spots may occur.
  • Once opened, formula should be used within 21 days. Any unopened formula should not be used after the expiration date printed by the manufacturer on the box.
